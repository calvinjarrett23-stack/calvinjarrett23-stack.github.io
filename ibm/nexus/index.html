IBM Consulting — Case study

NEXUS

Find the right internal expert in seconds, instead of guessing who to ask.

Problem

IBM employees — interns especially — had no reliable way to find the right internal expert for a given question. The knowledge existed somewhere in the organization; figuring out who held it was guesswork.

What was built

NEXUS is an AI knowledge routing platform: a full-stack RAG application built on React, Node, and PostgreSQL with pgvector, integrated with IBM ICA. It routes questions to internal experts with confidence scoring, surfaces the organization's expertise as an interactive knowledge-graph visualization, and includes a PowerPoint generation pipeline.

My specific role

I led the team and did all of the development myself.

Outcome

Presented at IBM's intern competition; no formal placement or result yet.
