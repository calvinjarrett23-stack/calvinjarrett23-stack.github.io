IBM Consulting — Case study

Predictive Cash Flow

Replaced a manual, unreliable cash-forecasting process with an automated, rules-driven model.

Problem

the client needed better forward visibility into cash. Forecasting was manual and spreadsheet-based — slow to produce and unreliable.

What was built

A predictive daily cash flow solution in Oracle EPM, built in Calculation Manager on the OEP_DCSH BSO cube: day-of-week conditional cash-flow rules, SOFR loan-schedule tracking with interest-rate autofill, and beginning-cash-balance seeding.

My specific role

Built jointly with one other intern.

Outcome

Built and delivered to the client; adoption is still in progress.
