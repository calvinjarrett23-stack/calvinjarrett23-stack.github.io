Independent Work — Case study

weduediligence.com

Live product, paying client. Removes the guesswork foreclosure buyers face when bidding on a property they can't fully vet in time.

Problem

Foreclosure buyers had no fast way to vet a property before bidding. Existing diligence options were too slow or too expensive for auction timelines, so buyers leaned on incomplete public records — and bid on properties they couldn't fully assess.

What was built

A paywalled, Zillow-like listing platform for foreclosure properties, combining automated data pulls, manual entry and review, and an AI assessment of each property.
