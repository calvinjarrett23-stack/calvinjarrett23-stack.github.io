Case study — leveraged recapitalization

What happens if Costco levers up?

Costco today: financed almost entirely with equity. A+ rated, roughly $15B of cash on hand.

Issue $5.0B of investment-grade notes at 5.5% and deploy it into a buyback…

…the tax shield adds $1.25B of value, and at an illustrative 80/20 mix WACC falls from 7.79% toward 6.7%…

…but at $5B the trade is EPS-dilutive: −1.43%, even with 5.0M shares retired.
